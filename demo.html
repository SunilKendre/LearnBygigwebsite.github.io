ABOUT US

LearnByGigs is a Telegram Bot. The main aim of LearnByGigs bot is to help students to learn by performing gigs, also get an idea of working on real life projects. This bot will allow CTOs or project owners to update or upload their project requirements, and hence we can call them Gigs.

We provide a platform where CTOs or project owners upload their project requirements, we can call them Gigs then students can take up those gigs and learn by completing those gigs.

What are Gigs?

Things or tasks or a requirement which we do Eg. Swiggy delivery, amazon delivery, ola driving etc. are a sort of gig. These are physical gigs, similarly we have technical gigs or programming gigs. So gigs exists in all domain.

LearnByGigs is targeted only for tech students and tech professionals will be able to launch their gigs on this platform. While students take up those gigs and learn by completing those gigs and in the process they will also get an idea of working on real life projects because the industry persons will upload it here.

We provide multiple gigs to perform and learn . Performing these gigs help you to actually understand that how things are implemented in a project. Submitting all gigs will improve your programming skills.
